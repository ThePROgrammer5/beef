Featured Recipe: Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Discover the secrets to making a perfect, creamy spaghetti carbonara.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Cheese
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, then remove the garlic.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino, Parmesan, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some cooking water. Toss the pasta in the pancetta pan and remove from heat.
  5. Stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved water to make it creamy.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
